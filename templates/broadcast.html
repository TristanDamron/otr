Open Technology Radio

How to broadcast

Open Technology Radio relies on a strong community to reach as many people as possible. One way to help us grow is to broadcast on a local radio frequency with a Raspberry Pi. If you are interested, please click here to download the OTR Broadcaster Kit to get started!

Other ways to help

If you like what you've been listening to on OTR, then getting your friends to tune in to a show is a great way to help the community grow. Another way is to become an active part of the OTR IRC; it's a great way to make new friends and share your thoughts on the shows. If you are a developer and want to make OTR better, visit the Open Technology Radio Github page and contribute to the repository.

Thanks for listening and being awesome!
